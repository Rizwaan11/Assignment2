Frequently Asked Questions

What is a To-Do App?

▼

A To-Do App is a tool that helps you organize and manage tasks and to-do lists in a digital format. It helps users to stay organized and be more productive.

How do I add tasks?

▼

To add a task, simply go to the "Add Task" section, enter the task description, set a deadline if required, and click "Add". Your task will appear in your task list.

Can I edit my tasks?

▼

Yes, you can easily edit your tasks by clicking on the task you want to update. You can change the task description, deadline, or status.

Is there a way to mark tasks as completed?

▼

Absolutely! Once you finish a task, simply click on the checkmark or "Complete" button next to the task to mark it as completed.
